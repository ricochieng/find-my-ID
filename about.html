About


This website acts a central repository for submitting and finding lost identification cards.

The motivation behind creating this website is to eliminate the agony and anguish that Kenyan citizens go through when they lose their ID's, the inability to perform routine daily tasks such as operating an Mpesa account is near impossible without this crucial document.

We aim to enabling people to register and retrieve their ID cards with an estimated turnaround time of at least a week.
